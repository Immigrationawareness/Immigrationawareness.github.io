OUR MISSION

This summer we participated in Girls Who Code, a non-profit organization working towards closing the gender gap in computer science. We participated in the Summer Immersion program at NJIT. As our final project we decided to build a website that not only provides the resources to help immigrants who come to the U.S., but also raise immigration awareness in the U.S.
